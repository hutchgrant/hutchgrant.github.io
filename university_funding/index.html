Methodology

Data was extracted from the Canadian Government's website for the Office of the Commissioner of Lobbying. This was done using bash tools and LibreOffice to: extract, format, calculate the data.

Problems

Universities that had no data on lobbying include:

  • Nipissing University
  • Laurentian University
  • Carleton University
  • Algoma University

Not impressed with the Ontario Government's lobbying site. Their was little data and many lobbyists registrations weren't updated after 2011.
